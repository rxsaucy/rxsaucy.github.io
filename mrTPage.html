Welcome to The Choral Dashboard

The Choral Dashboard is a centralized platform designed to help choir members access practice materials, sheet music, schedules, and important resources all in one place.

What We Do

This application provides:

  • A calendar to view upcoming rehearsals and events
  • Access to practice tracks through Choral Player
  • The official choral handbook
  • Sheet music library from Google Drive
  • Additional resources and help materials

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 18, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Choral Dashboard ("we," "our," or "the Application") handles information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. The Choral Dashboard operates entirely in your web browser and does not have any backend servers or databases.

The only data stored is:

  • Theme Preference: Your choice of light or dark mode is saved in your browser's local storage. This is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Embedded Content

This application displays content from external sources through embedded iframes. When you view these embedded resources:

  • You interact directly with the third-party service (Google Drive, Google Docs, Choral Player)
  • Any authentication or data collection is handled by those services, not by this application
  • We have no access to or control over any data you provide to these embedded services
  • Each embedded service operates under its own privacy policy

Third-Party Services

This application integrates with the following third-party services. Each service operates under its own privacy policy and terms of service:

Google Drive:

  • Used to display sheet music files from a shared folder through an embedded iframe
  • Any sign-in or authentication happens directly with Google within the iframe
  • We do not have access to your Google credentials or account information
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Terms of Service: https://policies.google.com/terms

Google Docs:

  • Used to embed and display the choral handbook
  • No authentication required; document is publicly accessible
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Choral Player (app.choralplayer.com):

  • Embedded third-party application for practice tracks
  • Any data collection is governed by Choral Player's own privacy policy
  • We do not control or have access to data collected by Choral Player

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. The only browser storage used is:

  • Local Storage: To remember your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Session Storage: Google OAuth may use session storage for authentication state during sign-in

We do not use analytics, advertising cookies, or any tracking technologies.

Data Security

Since we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any servers, there is no centralized database that could be compromised. All data remains:

  • In your browser's local storage (theme preference only)
  • In your browser's memory during your session (OAuth tokens)
  • Within Google's secure infrastructure (your Drive files)

We recommend using a secure, up-to-date web browser and keeping your Google account credentials private.

Children's Privacy

This application may be used by students of various ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or any age. Since we do not collect any personal information at all, there is no risk of collecting children's data. However, use of the Google Drive integration requires a Google account, which has its own age requirements under Google's Terms of Service.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Access: Since we don't store your data, there is nothing to access or export
  • Deletion: You can clear your theme preference by clearing your browser's local storage
  • Third-Party Services: Any data shared with embedded services (Google Drive, Choral Player) is governed by their respective privacy policies

International Users

This application can be accessed from anywhere in the world. Since no data is collected or transmitted to any servers controlled by us, there are no international data transfers to disclose. Any data processed by Google services (Drive, Docs) is subject to Google's privacy policy and their international data transfer practices.
